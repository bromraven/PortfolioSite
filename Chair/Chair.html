Adirondak Recliner

CNC Birch Plywood Chair

Nov 4 - Dec 16 2025

Ideated and prototyping using scale models, ran CNC cut plywood, constructed and finished chair.

Given a single sheet of three quarter inch birch plywood I developed a recliner that was relatively comfortable in both a prone and relaxed positions, while keeping construction as simple and easy as possible. I sourced the ergonomic proportions and measurements from the Measure of Man ergonomic study by Henry Dreyfuss in 1966.

This chair project was made of a single sheet of birch plywood with a CNC Router. I wanted a recliner that was relatively comfortable in both prone and relaxed positions, While being as simple and easy to construct as possible. I used the airline seat dimensions from the 1966 study Measure of Man ergonomic study.
